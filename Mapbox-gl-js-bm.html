Worcester, Massachusetts, USA

This is a map I created for the Regional Environmental Council of Worcester with Mapbox Studio. Because REC is a small non-profit and does not have a style guide, I found the colors in their logo by taking a screenshot of the logo and using a hex color picker tool. The colors incorporated from the logo are #9bc184 for the lightest green, #43a34a for the middle green, and #006324 for the darkest green. I work at this organization as a manager of the mobile market program!
